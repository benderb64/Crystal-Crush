Crystal Crush

How to Play...

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game, "Random Number"
  • The 4 crystals are in order below:
  • Atacamite
  • Ruby
  • Amber
  • Moldavite
  • By clicking on the crystal you'll be adding random points to your score
  • You win the game by matching your score to the random number
  • You lose if your score goes over the random number
  • The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it
  • The crystal values reset after each game
